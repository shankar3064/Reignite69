About This Event

Join our gentle morning Tai Chi session at the beautiful East Coast Park! This beginner-friendly class is perfect for seniors looking to improve balance, flexibility, and overall well-being in a peaceful outdoor setting.

Our certified instructor, Master Chen Wei Ming, has over 20 years of experience teaching Tai Chi specifically to seniors. The session focuses on slow, flowing movements that are easy on the joints while providing excellent health benefits.

What to expect:

  • Gentle warm-up exercises
  • Basic Tai Chi forms and movements
  • Breathing techniques for relaxation
  • Cool-down and meditation

All fitness levels welcome! Bring a water bottle and wear comfortable clothing. Mats will be provided.
